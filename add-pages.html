Add pages to your blog

October 29, 2019 by Binny Kanjur

xStatic comes with two content types, posts & pages. Pages are static contents that are not listed with your blog posts. About & Contact are examples of a Page.

Add a Post

  • Navigate to the Pages screen.
  • Click the +(plus) button to add a new page.

Compose Pages

In the Compose Page screen, fill in the Title, Slug, page body content, excerpt & select a featured image for the page.

Compose About page.

  1. In the Title field, enter About.
  2. Slug will be automatically generated.
  3. Copy the contents (Markdown) from About.md into the Page Body section.
  4. Provide What is Lorem Ipsum? in the Excerpt field.
  5. Select lorem-ipsum-star-wars.jpg for the Featured Image.
  6. To save the page, click Save.
  7. Navigate to the Pages screen by click the Back <-- button.
